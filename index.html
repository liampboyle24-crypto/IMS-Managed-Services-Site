Install Day Is Not the Finish Line.

Most AV programs are built to succeed on day one. Not day one thousand.

The hard part is not buying the room. The hard part is keeping every room reliable, visible, supported, and ready for the next budget cycle.

Year 1

It works. No one is watching it.

Year 2

Year 4

Refresh shows up as a surprise capital request, not a plan.
