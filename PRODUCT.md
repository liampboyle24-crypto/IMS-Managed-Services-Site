# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Partners, prospects, and internal leadership evaluating IMS as an AV managed-services operator — IT directors, facilities/AV leads, and executives who need to trust a vendor with boardroom and meeting-room technology before engaging further. They arrive skeptical of generic AV vendors and are scanning for credibility signals (clarity, restraint, accountability) more than for feature lists.

## Product Purpose

A single-page B2B marketing site that converts visiting partners/prospects into contacts via the top CTA, which scrolls to the on-page contact section (no separate assessment tool or workflow exists). Success looks like: a partner or prospect reads it in one sitting, understands IMS runs AV as an ongoing operating model rather than installing it once, and reaches out to lboyle@imsts.com.

## Brand Personality

Executive-ready, premium but not gimmicky, clear and credible, conversion-focused, modern without reading as generic AI-generated SaaS design. Confidence expressed through restraint — structured layout and strong typography do the persuading, not decoration. Voice is direct and accountable ("we run it," not "we install it").

## Anti-references

No generic AI-generated visuals or stock-feeling imagery. No gradient overuse, no excessive glassmorphism, no low-contrast text. No fake metrics, awards, certifications, or client names — only claims already present or explicitly provided. No bloated libraries or dependencies the site doesn't need. No design trends chosen for novelty over durability (avoid anything that will look dated in a year).

## Design Principles

- **Operator, not vendor.** Every section should reinforce ongoing accountability and partnership, never a one-time install-and-leave.
- **Earned credibility.** Never fabricate proof. If a claim isn't already supported by existing copy or provided material, it doesn't go on the page.
- **Executive attention span.** Write and lay out for a leadership audience skimming on a constrained time budget — clear hierarchy, strong CTAs, no filler.
- **Restraint over spectacle.** Premium reads as confidence. Motion, imagery, and visual flourish must each justify their presence; default to removing rather than adding.
- **One canonical surface.** `index.html` at the repo root is the single live deliverable a browser actually loads. The other `index_*.html` files in this repo are archived comparison drafts from earlier exploratory passes, not parallel products to keep in sync.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA baseline: body text ≥4.5:1 contrast, large/bold text ≥3:1, full keyboard navigation, and `prefers-reduced-motion` honored across all animation and parallax effects (already implemented site-wide as of the current pass). No additional named user needs specified.
